Beware of black ice, slick conditions on area roads All things considered, road conditions could have been a lot worse following the light snow that came down during the Friday evening rush hour. But Saturday morning the danger is the potential for black ice and other slick spots. In Maryland, Montgomery and Prince George's Counties have 275 salt and sand trucks out. ABC7's Richard Reeve reports from Potomac, Maryland on conditions around the D.C. area and what to expect Saturday morning.
